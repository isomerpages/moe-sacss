<h4>SACSS Care Fund -</h4>
<p><a href="https://form.gov.sg/66ecdb458f79422c6d574999" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://form.gov.sg/66ecdb458f79422c6d574999</a>
</p>
<p>SACSS Care Fund provides short to medium term additional financial aid
to students in</p>
<p>SACSS who are under the school's financial assistance scheme.</p>
<p><strong><u>Eligibility Criteria:</u></strong>
</p>
<p>• Beneficiaries of the school financial assistance scheme, including Ministry
of Education (MOE) Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS), non-MOE FAS or Saint
Magdalene-MILK Bursary (MILK) and other similar financial assistance schemes
that SACSS has in place with external organisations.</p>
<p>• Open to Singapore Citizen, Permanent Residents and International Students
who meets the eligibility criteria</p>
<h4>Grace Chew Compassion Fund -</h4>
<p>&nbsp;<a href="https://form.gov.sg/66f0dd7c0600a84ba7455d84" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://form.gov.sg/66f0dd7c0600a84ba7455d84</a>
</p>
<p>Applicant must be a current student of St Anthony's Canossian Secondary
School. Open to all Singaporeans, Singapore PRs and International Students.</p>
<p><strong><u>Eligibility Criteria:</u></strong>
</p>
<p>- Application is open to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents and International</p>
<p>Students who :</p>
<p>a. are facing immediate hardship from an emergency, accident, or other
unexpected critical incident, resulting in family loss of income;</p>
<p>b. require financial assistance to pay for expenses which are unexpected,
unforeseen, urgent, and unavoidable;</p>
<p>c. have completed application in full, subject to review and assessment
by panel; and</p>
<p>d. meets the eligibility criteria which include:</p>
<p>i. Family must face a loss of income due to a crisis;</p>
<p>ii. Family must have school going children;</p>
<p>iii. Family must have limited financial resources to cope with crisis;
and</p>
<p>iv. Meets the minimum of 80% school attendance by the student</p>
<h4>Thio Ying Ying Bursary Award -</h4>
<p>&nbsp;<a href="https://form.gov.sg/684a65dd5d6656cfcccfcc55" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://form.gov.sg/684a65dd5d6656cfcccfcc55</a>
</p>
<p>Thio Ying Ying Bursary Award is an award to dignify and encourage active
volunteerism in students from low-income families. Through this award,
the school hopes to instill a sense of gratitude in our students, to inspire
them to be gracious, and to strive for greater achievements benefiting
themselves and the community. Applicant must be a current student of St
Anthony's Canossian Secondary School.</p>
<p><strong><u>Objectives</u></strong>
</p>
<p>1. To provide financial assistance to students from low income families
(regardless of nationalities) so as to encourage them to live out the school’s
mission statement to serve others;</p>
<p>2. To nurture youth leaders who can adopt an entrepreneurial and innovation-focused
approach to community issues through training and mentoring to give back
to the community; and</p>
<p>3. To complement the role and function of the Ministry of Education (MOE)
and available community financial assistance scheme for needy students.</p>
