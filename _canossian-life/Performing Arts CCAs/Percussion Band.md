<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p>The Percussion Band brings a vibrant pulse to SACSS events, energising
occasions from formal ceremonies to festive celebrations. As one of the
few school‑based percussion bands CCA in Singapore, members explore a diverse
range of instruments, from pitched percussion such as marimbas to unpitched
instruments like drums, demonstrating creativity, teamwork, and musicality.</p>
<p>Their strong presence across both school and national platforms reflects
a tradition of excellence, shaped by dedication, unity, and a shared passion
for music, as they continue to inspire audiences and fellow performers
alike.</p>
