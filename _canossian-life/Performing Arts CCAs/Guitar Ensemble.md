<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p>The Guitar Ensemble of St. Anthony’s Canossian has been striking a chord
since 1996. Through dedication, discipline, and a love for guitar music,
members bring melodies to life, captivating audiences at annual Arts Alive
concerts and the Guitar Ensemble Festival. In 2025, the ensemble earned
a Certificate of Distinction at the Singapore Youth Festival and swept
top honours at the 17th Guitar Ensemble Festival, including Overall Outstanding
Ensemble and Best Conductor Award. More than notes and strings, the ensemble
celebrates the power of music to inspire, connect, and move audiences,
fostering creativity, teamwork, and passion among its members.</p>
