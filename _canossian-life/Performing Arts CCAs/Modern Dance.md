<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>SAC Modern Dance provides students with a vibrant platform to explore
creativity, movement, and self-expression through dance. Members develop
strong foundations in modern dance techniques while learning to perform
with confidence and artistry. Through regular training, performances, and
competitions, dancers cultivate discipline, teamwork, and resilience. The
CCA encourages students to express stories and emotions through movement
while working collaboratively as a team. Together, they grow as performers
and individuals, celebrating creativity and the joy of dance.</p>
