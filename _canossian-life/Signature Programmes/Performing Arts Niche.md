<h2><strong>Nurturing the Heart, Stage, and Soul: Performing Arts at SACSS</strong></h2>
<p>The Performing Arts at St. Anthony’s Canossian Secondary School (SACSS)
is more than just a tradition; it is a legacy of excellence that dates
back to the early 1950s. What began as year-end concerts and Christmas
plays has evolved into a comprehensive, forward-thinking <strong>Performing Arts Education (PfE)</strong> niche
that empowers every Canossian to lead and serve with grace.</p>
<p></p>
<hr>
<p></p>
<h3><strong>Our Heritage: From Classroom to Center Stage</strong></h3>
<p>In 1998, inspired by the transformative power of drama, our former Principal,
Sister Cecily Pavri, integrated the Performing Arts into the formal curriculum.
This visionary move culminated in 2004 with the official launch of our
niche area and the birth of our signature biennial festival, <strong><em>Arts Alive!</em></strong> Today,
our PfE curriculum—comprising <strong>Music and Drama</strong> for all Lower
Secondary students—is designed to nurture interest, build self-confidence,
and expose students to a diverse world of artistic expression. This foundation
is further strengthened by our eight Performing Arts CCAs, which consistently
achieve top honors at the Singapore Youth Festival and prestigious international
competitions.</p>
<p></p>
<hr>
<p></p>
<h3><strong>Empowering Future-Ready Canossians</strong></h3>
<p>As the landscape of education and the creative industries evolves, so
does SACSS. Our department vision is <strong>to empower future-ready Canossians of influence through performing arts</strong>,
fostering creativity, resilience, and leadership rooted in compassion and
competence.</p>
<p>We are committed to a curriculum that aligns with our school vision of
producing "Canossians of Influence" who <strong>Lead and Serve</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Passion &amp; Pathways:</strong> To support students eyeing careers
in the creative industries, we have established strategic partnerships
with <strong>LASALLE College of the Arts</strong> since 2025. Students gain
direct insight from industry professionals and experience the rigors of
higher arts education.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Project-Based Learning (PBL):</strong> Through intentional Project-Based
Learning, students become agents of their own discovery, building the resilience
and critical thinking skills necessary for the 21st century.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Cognitive Growth &amp; 21CC:</strong> We have enhanced our Schemes
of Work (SOW) to focus explicitly on <strong>21st Century Competencies</strong> and <strong>Metacognition</strong>.
By reflecting on their own creative processes, students develop a deeper
understanding of how they learn and create.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Art for the Community:</strong> Our students don’t just perform
for applause; they perform for a purpose. Through <strong>Values-in-Action (VIA)</strong> and <strong>Service Learning</strong>,
students use their artistic talents to build community and serve others,
embodying the Canossian spirit.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<hr>
<p></p>
<h3><strong>A Gateway to the Creative World</strong></h3>
<p>The SACSS PfE niche provides a forward-thinking foundation to meet the
demands of modern arts schools and the creative economy. Beyond the classroom,
our students engage in:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Industry Immersions:</strong> Visits to <strong>SOTA</strong>, the <strong>University of the Arts Singapore</strong>,
and various creative industry hubs.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Professional Mentorship:</strong> Talks and workshops led by practicing
professionals to bridge the gap between school and the professional stage.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>"At SACSS, we provide a holistic performing arts education that nurtures
artistic growth and 21st Century Competencies, preparing students to lead,
collaborate, and serve with passion and purpose."</p>
<p>Whether our students choose to grace professional stages or pursue other
paths, they leave SACSS equipped with the <strong>creativity, collaboration, and communication</strong> skills
to influence the world for the better.</p>
