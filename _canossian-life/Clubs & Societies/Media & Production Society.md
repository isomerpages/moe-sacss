<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The Media and Production Society (MPS) equips students with photography,
videography and production skills to support school events and assemblies.
Members focus on either capturing moments at carnivals, masses and competitions,
or managing sound, lighting and staging for school activities. Daily morning
assembly duties teach teamwork, responsibility and attention to detail.
During events, production, photography and videography teams collaborate
closely to ensure smooth execution and timely completion of projects. Participation
in competitions such as <em>Our Schools, Our Story</em>&nbsp;and the Objectifs
Student Visual Storytelling Awards further develops creativity, confidence
and collaboration, helping students grow as skilled, resourceful and resilient
contributors to the school community.</p>
