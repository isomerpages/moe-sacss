<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p>The SAC Girl Guides Company continues to shine as a vibrant force of leadership,
service and sisterhood. Rooted in the Canossian tradition and guided by
the global Girl Guide mission, it provides a safe and empowering space
for young women to grow as leaders and changemakers. Members embark on
a journey of friendship, resilience and confidence, learning through badgework,
leadership programmes, camps and community projects. Our signature programmes
include the annual outdoor camp at Camp Christine and championing causes
through the Advocacy Mentorship Programme. Aspiring girl guides can also
challenge themselves by working for the Baden-Powell Award and the President’s
Guide Award.</p>
