<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>In NCDCC, our cadets grow not just as responders but as compassionate
and values-driven individuals. Through hands-on training in first aid,
drills, and civil defence knowledge, they build confidence, resilience,
and discipline. Guided by Canossian values of love, service, and humility,
cadets learn to look beyond themselves and care for others. The unit is
strengthened by a supportive network of alumni who mentor and journey with
them. Key experiences such as Service Month and community outreach projects
provide meaningful opportunities to serve. Through these, cadets learn
to lead with purpose, serve with heart, and make a positive difference
in the community.</p>
