<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Established in 2014, the SACSS Track and Field CCA provides a dynamic
platform for students to discover and maximise their athletic potential
across a diverse range of events, from cross-country and sprints to jumps.
Through structured and progressive training, students not only develop
physical excellence but also build mental strength, resilience, and competitive
spirit—hallmarks of outstanding sportspersons.</p>
<p>Beyond performance, members cultivate determination, discipline, and a
passion for excellence that extends far beyond their school years. The
programme places strong emphasis on character development, sportsmanship,
and self-discipline, shaping confident individuals who strive to excel
both on and off the track.</p>
