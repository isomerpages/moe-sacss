<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>Taekwondo at SACSS is a journey of discipline, resilience and respect.
Members train in both Poomsae and Kyorugi (sparring), developing strength,
flexibility and precision, while also building confidence, perseverance
and teamwork. Training sessions strike a balance between discipline and
fun, with strong bonds formed through shared effort and encouragement.
Over the years, the team has consistently excelled, achieving multiple
medals at the National School Games Taekwondo Championships, a proud achievement
that reflects their dedication, unity and fighting spirit.</p>
