<p><strong><u>Books, Stationery, Uniform &amp; PE Attire Purchase Information (For non-MOE FAS)</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Dear Parent / Guardian,</p>
<p>You may access the booklists via</p>
<p><a href="https://www.stanthonyscanossiansec.moe.edu.sg/files/One%20Stop/Booklist/S1.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.stanthonyscanossiansec.moe.edu.sg/files/One%20Stop/Booklist/S1.pdf</a>&nbsp;</p>
<p>All parents / guardians <strong>MUST</strong> order books, stationery, uniform
and PE attire online via Pacific Bookstore's website (<a href="https://www.pacificbookstores.com" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.pacificbookstores.com</a>)&nbsp;</p>
<p>Online booking is available now and you may select the collection date
or arrange for home delivery via the website.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><u>Considerations when purchasing Textbooks and Uniforms:</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Textbooks</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Do be mindful to purchase the textbooks according to the academic level
that your daughter/ward will be taking for each subject.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Uniforms</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>All students will wear their full school uniform (school blouse, skirt,
tie and name tag) on Mondays and school official events. From Tuesdays
to Fridays, they have the option to wear their blue PE-shirt with their
skirts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Typically, a student will purchase at least 1 school blouse, 3 skirts,
3 blue PE-shirts, 3 PE-shorts, 1 tie and 3 or 4 pairs of school white socks/white
socks (assuming regular washing of clothes is done within the week).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>SACSS Name Tag</p>
<p>The school will notify your daughter to collect and pay for her name tag
from the bookshop by February 2025.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
