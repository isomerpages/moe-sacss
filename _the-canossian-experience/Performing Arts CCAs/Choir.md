<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>SAC Choir brings heart and harmony to SACSS, performing across school
events and beyond. As one of SAC's most spirited CCAs, members develop
their vocal technique across a range of choral styles, from classical to
contemporary, building musicality, discipline, and confidence along the
way.</p>
<p>Beyond the stage, SAC Choir is a close-knit community where every voice
is valued. Through collaborative rehearsals and shared performances, members
grow together — supporting one another and singing as one family. Their
dedication and unity continue to inspire audiences, embodying the choir's
belief that music is best made together.</p>
