<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p>In the Angklung Band, music is more than sound—it is a bond that unites
students. Every chime of the angklung and beat of the drums creates a symphony
of creativity, passion, and teamwork. Dedicated teachers and instructors
nurture members’ talents, fostering growth as both musicians and individuals.
In 2025, the band’s unique blend of traditional and contemporary rhythms
captivated audiences at the Arts Alive festival and earned a Certificate
of Distinction at the Singapore Youth Festival. Members take pride in being
part of this musical family, where every performance celebrates their unity,
spirit, and love for music.</p>
