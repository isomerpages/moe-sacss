<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p>The SAC Drama Club is a vibrant and dynamic CCA that provides students
with a platform to explore creativity, build confidence, and express themselves
through the performing arts. Through engaging workshops, improvisation
exercises, and full-scale productions, members develop essential skills
such as communication, collaboration, and critical thinking. The club fosters
a supportive environment where students are encouraged to take risks, experiment
with ideas, and grow both on and off the stage.</p>
<p>Our commitment to excellence is reflected in the club’s outstanding achievements
at the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF), where we have been honoured with
the Distinction Award for many consecutive years. This consistent recognition
is a testament to the dedication, discipline, and passion of our students
and teachers. Beyond accolades, Drama Club nurtures resilience and teamwork,
shaping students into confident individuals who are able to connect with
audiences and bring stories to life with authenticity and hear!.</p>
