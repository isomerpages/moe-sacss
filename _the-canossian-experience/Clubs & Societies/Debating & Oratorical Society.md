<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p>The Debating and Oratorical Society (DOS) is a dynamic Co-Curricular Activity
that equips students with essential skills in communication, critical thinking,
and public speaking. Through debates, speeches, and wordplay challenges,
members develop confidence, clarity, and composure.</p>
<p>Students participate in both inter-school competitions and internal events,
showcasing creativity, resilience, and persuasive ability. Beyond these
platforms, DOS fosters teamwork, respect, and humility, nurturing students
into thoughtful leaders and empathetic communicators.</p>
