<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p>Rooted in the Canossian values of compassion, service, and inclusivity,
St Magdalene’s Club empowers members to discover their potential to inspire
and do good. The club develops creativity, confidence, and character through
hands-on projects in the arts, culinary skills, and social enterprise.
In these student-led initiatives, members collaborate to think critically,
serve meaningfully, and positively impact the school and wider community.
More than a CCA, the club is a nurturing community where members care for
one another, embrace diversity, and grow into women of purpose, fostering
dignity, respect, and service in line with the SAC vision.</p>
