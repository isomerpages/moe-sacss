<p><strong>ABOUT</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>The SACSS Library plays a vital role in nurturing a love for reading and
learning among students. Its mission is to inspire curiosity, encourage
critical thinking, and promote reading as a lifelong habit. Annual events
such as the Top Readers’ Party celebrate avid readers, while the Literature
Quiz sparks interest in diverse genres and authors. Students also develop
digital literacy through contests like SURE by the National Library Board.
The library invests in its librarians with enrichment activities and cultural
experiences, deepening their appreciation for literature and the arts.
Through these efforts, the library fosters a vibrant, curious, and literate
school community.<strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
