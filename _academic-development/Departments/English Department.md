<p><strong>Objectives (short paragraph)</strong>
</p>
<p>
<br>
</p>
<p>The English Language and Literature (EL/Lit) Department aims to develop
students into <strong>Empathetic Communicators, Discerning Readers and Creative Inquirers</strong>.
Through the study of language and literature, students build a strong foundation
in communication while learning to think critically and respond thoughtfully
to the world around them.</p>
<p>The department also seeks to nurture students who are aware of global
issues and sensitive to diverse perspectives. In doing so, students grow
into <strong>concerned global citizens and active, adaptive contributors</strong>,
while developing a deeper interest and confidence in both English Language
and Literature.</p>
<p></p>

<p><strong>Approach (bulleted points)</strong>
</p>
<p>Our teaching approach emphasises:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Explicit instruction of language skills and knowledge to establish strong
foundations in the English language.
<br>
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Exposure to a rich language environment that encourages meaningful reading,
writing, speaking and listening.
<br>
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The exploration of contemporary issues affecting Singapore and the wider
world, allowing students to apply language skills in authentic and relevant
contexts.
<br>
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A student-centric curriculum that supports diverse abilities, interests
and learning styles.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through this approach, students are encouraged to read critically, express
themselves clearly and engage thoughtfully with different viewpoints.</p>
